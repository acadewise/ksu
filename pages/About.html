About us

The IoT as a Service Research Group is composed of a multidisciplinary team of faculty members and students from the College of Computing and Software Engineering and from the Southern Polytechnic College of Engineering and Engineering Technology. Dr. Valero, Dr. Shahriar, Dr. Zhao, and Dr. Zhang belong to the Department of Information Technology. Dr. Li is part of the Computer Engineering Department. The Students are comprised of individuals from both colleges and multiple departments within each. The IoT as a Service Research Group is located on the Marietta Campus in the Atrium Building.
